* Introduction
  GeneSpider is a package for easy construction of linerar
  models used to evaluate gene regulatory network (GRN)
  inference algorithms or for testing experimental condtions
  for preparation of live experimentation.
** Aim
   GeneSpider aims to be a simple pipeline procedure that
   can be utilised in the real world to benefit in
   hypothesis construction.
